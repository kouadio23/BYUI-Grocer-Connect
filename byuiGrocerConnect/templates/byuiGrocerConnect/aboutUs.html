About BYUI Grocer Connect

Connecting BYU-I students for a better grocery shopping experience.

Our Mission

At BYUI Grocer Connect, we aim to simplify and improve the grocery shopping process for the BYU-I student community. Our platform facilitates a unique peer-to-peer grocery shopping experience, enabling students to either list their grocery needs or volunteer to pick up groceries for others. This not only makes shopping more convenient but also fosters a sense of community and service among students.

Who We Are

We're a team of BYU-I students and alumni, brought together by our shared passion for technology and community service. We believe in leveraging technology to solve everyday challenges and make life a little easier for everyone around us.

Why Choose Us?

  • Community-Driven: Our platform is built by and for BYU-I students.
  • Convenience: Easily list your grocery needs or help out fellow students with their shopping.
  • Save Time & Money: Reduce the need for individual trips to the store and take advantage of group savings.

Contact Us

Have questions or suggestions? We'd love to hear from you. Reach out to us at contact@byuigrocerconnect.com.
